We are thrilled to welcome Selda Morina for this session, she is an innovation manager and has been running the REWE Start-up Lounge for five years - thus offering food start-ups the all-round carefree package for market entry. She enjoys working with start-ups, knows the logic of large companies and therefore has the ideal interface competence for a basis of trust at eye level.🤝 At our festival, she will sit on the jury of the pitch session for food start-ups. Be there live when food startups get their chance at a Rewe listing! The winning team will get an exclusive coaching on the topic: how to get into LEH and what do I need for a listing at REWE.
